# Diet

Type name: [Diet](./Diet.md)

Extends [CreativeWork](./CreativeWork.md) or [LifestyleModification](./LifestyleModification.md)

| Name | Type | Description |
| --- | --- | --- |
| dietFeatures | [Text](./Text.md) | Nutritional information specific to the dietary plan. May include dietary recommendations on what foods to avoid, what foods to consume, and specific alterations/deviations from the USDA or other regulatory body's approved dietary guidelines. |
| endorsers | [Organization](./Organization.md) or [Person](./Person.md) | People or organizations that endorse the plan. |
| expertConsiderations | [Text](./Text.md) | Medical expert advice related to the plan. |
| overview | [Text](./Text.md) | Descriptive information establishing the overarching theory/philosophy of the plan. May include the rationale for the name, the population where the plan first came to prominence, etc. |
| physiologicalBenefits | [Text](./Text.md) | Specific physiologic benefits associated to the plan. |
| proprietaryName | [Text](./Text.md) | Proprietary name given to the diet plan, typically by its originator or creator. |
| risks | [Text](./Text.md) | Specific physiologic risks associated to the plan. |
