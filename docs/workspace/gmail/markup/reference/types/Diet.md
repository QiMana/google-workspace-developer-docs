# Diet

Type name: [Diet](https://developers.google.com/workspace/gmail/markup/reference/types/Diet)

Extends [CreativeWork](https://developers.google.com/workspace/gmail/markup/reference/types/CreativeWork) or [LifestyleModification](https://developers.google.com/workspace/gmail/markup/reference/types/LifestyleModification)

| Name | Type | Description |
| --- | --- | --- |
| dietFeatures | [Text](https://developers.google.com/workspace/gmail/markup/reference/types/Text) | Nutritional information specific to the dietary plan. May include dietary recommendations on what foods to avoid, what foods to consume, and specific alterations/deviations from the USDA or other regulatory body's approved dietary guidelines. |
| endorsers | [Organization](https://developers.google.com/workspace/gmail/markup/reference/types/Organization) or [Person](https://developers.google.com/workspace/gmail/markup/reference/types/Person) | People or organizations that endorse the plan. |
| expertConsiderations | [Text](https://developers.google.com/workspace/gmail/markup/reference/types/Text) | Medical expert advice related to the plan. |
| overview | [Text](https://developers.google.com/workspace/gmail/markup/reference/types/Text) | Descriptive information establishing the overarching theory/philosophy of the plan. May include the rationale for the name, the population where the plan first came to prominence, etc. |
| physiologicalBenefits | [Text](https://developers.google.com/workspace/gmail/markup/reference/types/Text) | Specific physiologic benefits associated to the plan. |
| proprietaryName | [Text](https://developers.google.com/workspace/gmail/markup/reference/types/Text) | Proprietary name given to the diet plan, typically by its originator or creator. |
| risks | [Text](https://developers.google.com/workspace/gmail/markup/reference/types/Text) | Specific physiologic risks associated to the plan. |
