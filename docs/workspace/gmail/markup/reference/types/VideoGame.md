Type name: [VideoGame](https://developers.google.com/workspace/gmail/markup/reference/types/VideoGame)

| actors | [Person](https://developers.google.com/workspace/gmail/markup/reference/types/Person) | An actor, e.g. in tv, radio, movie, video games etc. Actors can be associated with individual items or with a series, episode, clip. |

| directors | [Person](https://developers.google.com/workspace/gmail/markup/reference/types/Person) | A director of e.g. tv, radio, movie, video games etc. content. Directors can be associated with individual items or with a series, episode, clip. |
| gamePlatform | [Text](https://developers.google.com/workspace/gmail/markup/reference/types/Text), [Thing](https://developers.google.com/workspace/gmail/markup/reference/types/Thing) or [URL](https://developers.google.com/workspace/gmail/markup/reference/types/URL) | The electronic systems used to play [video games](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Category:Video_game_platforms). |

| musicBy | [MusicGroup](https://developers.google.com/workspace/gmail/markup/reference/types/MusicGroup) or [Person](https://developers.google.com/workspace/gmail/markup/reference/types/Person) | The composer of the soundtrack. |
| playMode | [GamePlayMode](https://developers.google.com/workspace/gmail/markup/reference/types/GamePlayMode) | Indicates whether this game is multi-player, co-op or single-player. The game can be marked as multi-player, co-op and single-player at the same time. |
| trailer | [VideoObject](https://developers.google.com/workspace/gmail/markup/reference/types/VideoObject) | The trailer of a movie or tv/radio series, season, episode, etc. |
