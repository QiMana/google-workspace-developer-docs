# 2022 may

May, 2022Introducing the Google Meet Live Sharing SDKWe recently announced at Google I/O new functionality for app developers to leverage the Google Meet video conferencing product through our new Meet Live Sharing SDK. Users can now come together and share experiences with each other inside an app, such as streaming a TV show, queuing up videos to watch on YouTube, collaborating on a music playlist, joining in a dance party, or working out together though Google Meet. This SDK joins the large set of offerings available to developers under the Google Workspace Platform.Learn moreRecap of Google Workspace Platform sessionsWe had an amazing time at Google I/O 2022. Google Workspace developers connected with experts, got inspired, and expanded their knowledge.
