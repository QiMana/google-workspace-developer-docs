Learn more Solutions Spotlight Custom function: Calculate a tiered pricing discount If you offer a tiered pricing system for your customers, this custom function makes it easier to calculate the discount amounts for your prices. For a given total price, the script loops through the specified tiers in the tier pricing table. For each portion of the total price that falls within a tier, that portion is multiplied by the tier's associated percent value. The result is the sum of each tier's calculation.
