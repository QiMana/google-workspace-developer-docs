# Partner with Google

Google offers a Partner program for all Google Workspace for Education developers. We work closely with hundreds of developer partners to build a thriving EdTech ecosystem. Our goal is to make it easy for educators to access innovative and impactful tools alongside our products.

## Partnership journey

There are two partnership levels. You'll first become a Build partner in Partner Advantage, then join the Integrated Solutions Initiative to unlock more partnership benefits. Both programs provide tangible benefits to your organization and integrations with Google.

To get started:

- [Sign up](https://edu.google.com/intl/ALL_us/resources/get-started/find-a-partner/become-a-partner/) for the Partner Advantage program. Select the **Build** track under **Google Workspace**.
- Visit your new member profile.
- Complete Solution qualification. Submit proof of any [*eligible* Google Workspace for Education or Google Classroom](https://services.google.com/fh/files/misc/gfe_build_partner_eligible_integrations.pdf).
- (Optional) [Apply for the Integrated Solutions Initiative](https://www.partneradvantage.goog/GCPPRM/s/partneradvantageportallogin?language=en_US).

Eligibility for each level depends on the number of integrations with Google Workspace for Education that you have built:

- To qualify for Partner Advantage Build partner, you must build *at least one integration*
- To qualify for the Partner Advantage Education Integrated Solutions Initiative, you must meet the following requirements:
	- Build *at least three integrations*
		- Your product may not contain ads or student purchases
		- Your product needs to be COPPA/FERPA compliant (and GDPR compliant if available in EU)

## Program benefits

The Partner Advantage program offers a deeper connection to Google and cooperative opportunities to promote and market your products. Partners have access to a variety of valuable tools and information.

#### Build partners

- Access the development sandbox
- Exclusive support resources
	- Reach out to a dedicated Partner Advisor
		- Schedule technical office hours with the Google for Education Developer Relations Engineering team
		- Receive the community newsletter
- Unlock Marketing resources
	- Listing in Google's Partner Directory
		- Advertise your Google partnership

#### Education Integrated Solutions Initiative

Integrated Solutions Initiative members receive all of the Build partner benefits plus:

- Google for Education partner badge
- Branding toolkit access
- Listing in Google for Education [App Hub](https://edu.google.com/resources/get-started/apps/)
- Invitations to private education community & events
- Directly reach educators with product announcements through Google for Education channels
- Access Early Adopter Programs for new Classroom features

## Additional opportunities

The Google Workspace for Education Partner programs are continually expanding. We expect the following to become available for Partner Advantage members as the program expands:

- Tech demo webinar series
- Exclusive new feature previews
- Thought leadership opportunities
