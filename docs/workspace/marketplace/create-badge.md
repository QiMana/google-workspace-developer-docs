# Create a badge to promote your Google Workspace Marketplace app

To promote your Google Workspace Marketplace app in your digital marketing or communications assets, you can create the following HTML badge:

![A badge that reads Get it from the Google Workspace Marketplace.](https://workspace.google.com/static/img/marketplace/en/gwmBadge.svg)

## Badge guidelines

Follow these guidelines whenever you're using a Google Workspace Marketplace badge:

- Use the badges as provided. Never alter the badges.
- There must be clear space surrounding the badge equal to one-quarter the height of the badge.
- The badge must be large enough that all of the text is legible.
- The Google Workspace Marketplace badge should be the same size or larger than other application store badges.
- Badges must be shown on a solid colored background or a background image that doesn't obscure the badge.
- Match the badge language to the language of your marketing campaign whenever possible.
- Any online use of the badge must link to the Google Workspace Marketplace. Use the [badge generator](#generate-badge) to get the HTML to include in your digital marketing.
- The badge can only be used to promote content available on Google Workspace Marketplace.
- When there's space in the creative, use a legal attribution footer stating:
	"Google Workspace Marketplace and the Google Workspace Marketplace logo are trademarks of Google LLC."
- Never use the <sup>TM</sup> symbol in the legal attribution footer.

## Text usage guidelines

Follow these guidelines whenever you're using the Google Workspace Marketplace name in your marketing or communications:

- When mentioning content available from the Google Workspace Marketplace, always say "from the Google Workspace Marketplace.":
	- Incorrect: Our app is now available on the Google Workspace Marketplace.
		- Correct: Our app is now available from the Google Workspace Marketplace.
- Review [Google's branding guidelines](https://about.google/brand-resource-center/guidance/).
