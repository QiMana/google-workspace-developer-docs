# 1 December 2017

## Page Summary

- The schedule covers sessions and training on a variety of Google developer products and platforms including Android, Assistant, Cloud, Firebase, TensorFlow, Mobile Web, and more.
- Sessions cover topics such as Progressive Web Apps, Machine Learning with TensorFlow, building Android apps with Architecture Components, developing for the Google Assistant, and utilizing Firebase.
- The day includes dedicated time for breakfast, lunch, an afternoon break, and concludes with an After Party.
- Community-focused events are scheduled, including info sessions on the Community Groups and Google Developer Experts programs, and various community meetups.

All times are Indian Standard Time (UTC+05:30)
