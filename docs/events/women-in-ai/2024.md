# Women in AI Summit 2024

![](https://developers.google.com/static/events/women-in-ai/2024/images/wiai-yt1_2880.png)

### AI-powered transformation: Driving innovation and reshaping organizations

Explore AI's role in accelerating product development, enabling smarter decisions, and delivering personalized experiences. Learn how Google's AI ecosystem can help drive innovation within your organization.

![](https://developers.google.com/static/events/women-in-ai/2024/images/wiai-yt2_2880.png)

### Building with the Gemini API and Google AI Studio

Explore the power of Gemini APIs and Google AI Studio! Learn about key features like long context and fine-tuning, and see how the community is building innovative solutions. We'll also share resources to help you get started.

![](https://developers.google.com/static/events/women-in-ai/2024/images/wiai-yt3_2880.png)

### Introduction to Gemini APIs and Google AI Studio

Learn how to use the Gemini APIs and Google AI Studio to accelerate and automate your work. Build AI applications—no programming experience required.

![](https://developers.google.com/static/events/women-in-ai/2024/images/wiai-yt4_2880.png)

### AI for everyone with Gemma

Explore how Gemma, a family of powerful open models, is empowering developers to build the future. Discover community-driven innovation and the exciting potential of AI to solve humanity's great challenges—regardless of hardware limitations or geographic location.

![](https://developers.google.com/static/events/women-in-ai/2024/images/wiai-yt5_2880.png)

### AI in your pocket: Building intelligent Android apps

Dive into the future of mobile development with Gemini Nano, Flash and Pro! Explore how to integrate state-of-the-art on-device models directly into Android applications whether on-device or over the Cloud.

![](https://developers.google.com/static/events/women-in-ai/2024/images/wiai-yt6_2880.png)

### Prompt to production: Building an AI app with Flutter

Discover how to use Flutter to rapidly build and validate applications, accelerating the journey from idea to production. Prototype a generative AI solution, then build an app that leverages Vertex AI in Firebase.

![](https://developers.google.com/static/events/women-in-ai/2024/images/wiai-yt8_2880.png)

### How AI is changing the healthcare system

AI is revolutionizing the healthcare industry by offering innovative solutions to longstanding challenges. Explore how AI is enhancing diagnostic accuracy and reducing patient wait times.

![](https://developers.google.com/static/events/women-in-ai/2024/images/wiai-yt7_2880.png)

### Fast-tracking your AI career with Kaggle

Kaggle is a central hub for testing cutting-edge models and rapidly upskilling in data science. Learn how to leverage Kaggle's vast resources, participate in competitions, and translate success into career opportunities.

![](https://developers.google.com/static/events/women-in-ai/2024/images/wiai-yt9_2880.png)

### Assessing AI's progress

Get perspectives from our panel on the shifting dynamics, state of innovation, and opportunities for entrepreneurship in the evolving world of AI.
